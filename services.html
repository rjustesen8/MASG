Services

Athletic strategy services built around real school decisions.

MASG helps schools improve athletics visibility, planning clarity, and program-level decision support through focused reviews that are easier to understand and easier to use.

Service approach

Each service is framed around what changes for the school.

The goal is not to overwhelm a district with broad consulting language. The goal is to help school leaders better understand athletics pressure, support, and planning needs so they can make more disciplined next-step decisions.

Athletic financial visibility

Athletic Financial Review

Outcome: Give leaders a cleaner view of where athletics dollars are going and which areas are creating the most pressure.

This review organizes athletics finances into clearer categories so schools can move beyond broad assumptions and see how coaching support, uniforms, equipment, transportation, officials, facilities, insurance, and event operations are affecting the department.

Program-level decision support

Sport by Sport Analysis

Outcome: Help schools compare programs more clearly and make stronger choices about priorities, support, and resource allocation.

By looking at participation, cost, support, and resource use program by program, MASG helps schools see where pressure is concentrated, where support is stronger, and where a better balance may be needed.

Operational efficiency

Transportation and Operations Review

Outcome: Reveal where travel and athletics operations may be creating avoidable strain.

Travel is one of the most common hidden cost drivers in school athletics, especially for smaller and more rural contexts. This review helps schools think more clearly about mileage, scheduling impact, and contest-related operating pressures.

Outside support clarity

Booster and Fundraising Analysis

Outcome: Show where outside support is strong, uneven, or difficult to track across the department.

MASG helps schools understand how booster activity, fundraising, sponsorships, and outside support fit into the larger athletics picture so leaders can better judge sustainability and communication needs.

Community and program value

Attendance and Community Value Review

Outcome: Add context to athletics decisions by comparing more than cost alone.

Athletics matter because they shape student experience, school culture, and community connection. This review helps schools think more clearly about attendance, visibility, support, and broader program value alongside financial realities.

Long-term direction

Strategic Athletics Planning

Outcome: Move from one-year reactions toward a stronger planning structure.

MASG can organize findings into planning-ready summaries, recommendation frameworks, and a more strategic direction for leaders who want to build stronger, more sustainable athletic programs over time.

Current packaging

Pricing is intentionally lower right now.

MASG is in an early stage of experience building, so the current pricing is meant to reflect lower-risk entry points instead of pretending to be a fully mature consulting firm on day one.

That makes the site more believable, keeps the first step accessible, and gives schools a cleaner way to test whether the work would be useful.
